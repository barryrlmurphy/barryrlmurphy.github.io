Reading List

This is a list of some of the books I have read over the years.

Traditionally I would just buy and read a book but more recently I usually read a book summary first and then either buy the hard copy or listen to it on audible. This saves time and I find I get through a lot more valuable, interesting books this way. Please let me know if you have any comments or reading suggestions. Sorted by most recent to least.
